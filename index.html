The ClozeSure Completion Guarantee

If you are selling your home the ClozeSure Completion Guarantee guarantees the sale of your property if your buyer withdraws after the exchange of contracts. You will receive the price you agreed with your buyer promptly, meaning that you can secure your future and purchase your new home if you planned to do so. What's more, we'll cover any additional conveyancing and legal costs incurred as a result of your buyer withdrawing (up to £2,000).

ClozeSure makes your property sale certain from the moment of exchanging Contracts of Sale with your buyer... providing you with the immediate peace-of-mind and security that certainty brings!

Who is the ClozeSure Guarantee for?

The ClozeSure Guarantee is designed to protect owners who are selling their residential property in England, Wales or Northern Ireland.

It is available to buy after your property sale has been agreed and at the time of exchanging Contracts of Sale with your buyer, where:

Both buyer and seller are using the services of a conveyancer or solicitor.

Your buyer is a private individual.

A minimum deposit of 10% of the sale price has been received by your conveyancer/solicitor from your buyer.

The period between exchange and completion of contracts does not exceed 3 months.

More detailed information and answers to common questions are available in The Guarantee section, which we recommend you read.
