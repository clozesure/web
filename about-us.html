About Us

Our Business

The ClozeSure Property Contract Completion Guarantee is provided by Contract Completion Company Ltd (CCC), and secured in conjunction with our partners. CCC is an InsureTech startup company, merging many years of industry experience with the latest technology.

We were establised and registered in 2015 at 8-11 Crescent, London, EC3N 2LY, and are pre-IPO.

ClozeSure is a genuinely unique proposition

The ClozeSure Guarantee is unique in the market and meets a real need in protecting the sellors of properties in England and Wales. It has the potential to break the "property chain" problem that has dogged the market for years and provide peace of mind to our customers.



ClozeSure is built on latest generation technology

The ClozeSure platform is designed and built using the latest technology. Blockchain and advanced Artificial Intelligence algorythms provide enhanced security and risk management for our business and our customers, whilst our cloud infrastructure ensures reliability, performance and scalability.

Due to this, combined with a competitive pricing structure and an easy to use, easy to buy online experience, we anticipate high demand from end-customers and conveyancers and Estate Agents working on behalf of their clients. We have the business model, capital and technology in place to ensure we can meet this demand and fuel our success.



Our History

Our Founder, Peter, came upon the idea when a close friend found themselves almost ruined after a buyer for their property pulled out at the last moment. Peter couldn't believe that protection for this situation wasn't available so he searched for it but could not find a single provider - so we built it!
